The Theme Explained

Wholesomeness — God's
Restorative Nature

God's design is to make all things in alignment with His nature — which is Good. When something is broken, His nature moves not to condemn, but to restore, heal, and realign.

"The nature of sin makes and keeps things broken. The nature of God makes things whole."

Wholesomeness is not naïve optimism — it is the theological reality that Christ came to restore what sin fractured. In Him, our relationships, identities, and purpose are made complete.

This conference is an invitation to step into that completeness — spirit, soul, and body — together.

Meet the Conveners

Dare & Titi Med

Dare & Titi Med Conveners · Rock & Root Initiative

Dare and Titi Med are devoted followers of Jesus Christ and have been married for over 13 years. Their commitment to the ministry of the Gospel began during their university years, and since then they have served in various capacities — as support pastors, teachers, counselors, and ministry leaders.

They are deeply passionate about strengthening families, marriages, and relationships. Together, they lead SittingWithTheMeds, a platform for marriage coaching and mentoring, and host Worship, Chat & Chill — a Bible-study-centred community for honest conversations about faith. Dare and Titi are blessed with two wonderful children and are currently pursuing a Master's degree in Apologetics in the United States.
