About Us

Our Mandate & Founder's Story

There is a Creator. He made this world beautifully — sustaining every condition required for life to flourish. His nature is good: everything He made, Scripture confirms it was good. And His nature is to restore things back to working order.


Sin is a fallen nature — a perversion of what God made good. It attacks the most fundamental unit of society: the family. Unforgiveness, betrayal, broken unions, purposelessness — these are sin's fingerprints on God's design.


But God made provision to restore through Christ. Rock & Root Initiative is God's agent to propagate that solution. Our mandate is simple and eternal: to preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ and bring people into the knowledge of God's love — healing families and rebuilding what sin has torn down.

Anchor Scripture — Titus 2
"For the grace of God has appeared, bringing salvation for all people."

Our Vision

What We're Building Toward

A Good Creator

God exists and He is good. Everything He made was confirmed good in Scripture. His very nature is to restore — to bring what is broken back to life, purpose, and wholeness.

The Problem of Sin

Sin is a fallen nature that perverts God's good creation. It attacks the family — the most fundamental unit of society — through betrayal, unforgiveness, broken unions, death, and loss of purpose.

The Solution: Christ

God did not leave us without hope. He made provision through Jesus Christ to restore what sin destroyed. Rock & Root exists to carry that message and make it real in every life we touch.

The Conveners

Dare & Titi Med

Devoted followers of Jesus Christ, married for over 13 years, and passionate about strengthening families through the truth of the Gospel.

Dare & Titi Med
Conveners · Rock & Root Initiative

Dare and Titi's commitment to the ministry of the Gospel began during their university years. Since then they have served in various capacities — as support pastors, teachers, counselors, and ministry leaders. Together they lead SittingWithTheMeds, a platform for marriage coaching and mentoring, and host Worship, Chat & Chill — a Bible-study-centred community for honest conversations about faith. They are blessed with two wonderful children and are currently pursuing a Master's degree in Apologetics in the United States.

Dare Med
Technology & Innovation · Founder

A technology and innovation enthusiast with over 20 years of IT experience. Senior Solutions Architect at a global nonprofit in Canada. Holds a BSc from Ghana and an MBA from the University of South Wales. Founder of MyNamely and Brillare I.S., and co-founder of Milky Express.

Titi Med
IBCLC · Founder & CEO, Milky Express

International Board Certified Lactation Consultant and founder of Milky Express — supporting breastfeeding mothers through products, counselling, and education. Champion of maternal health across Nigeria and Africa. Holds a degree in Economics from KNUST.
